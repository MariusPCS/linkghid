In the middle of a heatwave, tower fans are among the most popular summer buys on eMAG — they are cheap, easy to move and start instantly. The ECG FS 91 T is one such popular model: a tower fan with ionisation, a remote control and quiet operation.

## What it offers

The tower format is slim and tall, so it takes up little floor space and tucks neatly beside a desk or bed. Oscillation moves air across the whole room, and the 3 speeds plus remote let you adjust everything from a distance. At 65 W, it draws far less power than an air conditioner.

## Fan or air conditioner?

Worth remembering: a fan moves air and creates a cooling sensation on your skin, but it does not lower the room's temperature. If you want a genuinely cooler room on scorching days, you need an air conditioner. For air circulation, more comfortable nights and a low running cost, though, a tower fan like this does the job.

## Verdict

For bedrooms, offices and small-to-medium rooms, the ECG FS 91 T is a practical, quiet choice with low power use. Manage your expectations versus an air conditioner and you'll be happy with it through the warm season.
