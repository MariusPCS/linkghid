# eMAG article

Follow the shared **`retailer-article`** workflow and common rules, plus these eMAG specifics.

- Program: <https://profitshare.ro/affiliate-programs/retail/emag> (re-verify; reviewed 2026-06-15)
- Merchant id: `emag` · Niche: general marketplace (electronics, home, appliances, fashion, …)
- Commission: **1–20%** by category · Attribution: **2–15 days** (mobiles/TVs/laptops = 2 days)

## eMAG-specific rules
- **No stated prices.** eMAG bars showing specific prices ("prețurile se modifică frecvent"); frame as
  indicative and send the reader to check the current price via the CTA. Leave `price` empty.
- **No coupon / cashback / discount-code framing**, and never publish "eMAG discount codes".
- **No eMAG trademark/logo misuse**; official product images only, or none (never dummy images).
- **Prohibited affiliate categories:** corporate/bulk orders, donations, medical surgical masks.
- Orders partly paid with a gift card earn commission only on the cash-paid product value.
